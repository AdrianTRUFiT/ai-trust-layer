# AI Fraud Prevention Checklist

## Identity

- Multi-factor authentication enabled
- Independent identity verification outside the primary platform
- Privileged account monitoring
- Device-level trust validation
- Vendor access review schedule

## Communication

- Verified communication archive
- Phishing escalation workflow
- AI impersonation awareness training
- Independent outbound notification channel

## Continuity

- Offline or local continuity snapshots
- Emergency operational recovery mode
- Vendor outage contingency planning
- Independent backup verification

## Vendor Dependency

- Vendor custody map completed
- Data concentration reviewed
- Independent audit trail retained
- API dependency review completed
- Platform failure impact assessed

## AI-Speed Threat Awareness

- AI-generated phishing simulations tested
- Rapid anomaly detection workflow documented
- Identity escalation process established
- High-risk communication triggers defined

## Governance

- Executive continuity review schedule
- Board-level dependency reporting
- Vendor incident response requirements
- Recovery accountability ownership defined
